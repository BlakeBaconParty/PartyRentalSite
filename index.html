About Us

Good News Party Rentals provides clean, fun, and family-friendly inflatables for birthdays, church events, and community gatherings. We focus on simple booking, good communication, and making your event easy from start to finish.

Pricing

Prices listed are maximum rates and may be lower depending on location, package, or multiple rentals. We aim to give you the best possible deal based on your event.

What to Expect

Once you book your inflatable, we typically arrive about one hour before your event to deliver and set everything up. We handle the setup, secure the inflatable with stakes or sandbags as needed, and test everything before we leave so you do not have to worry about the setup yourself.

Your quote includes delivery, setup, safety securing, and takedown after the event. We also clean the inflatable after use and put everything away afterward.

If you rent a generator, you will not need to set it up or add gasoline yourself. We will have it connected, tested, and ready to go as part of the setup.
